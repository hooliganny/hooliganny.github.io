The site

Welcome to my mini-blog, here I will post any significant updates to my portfolio. Each post will have corresponding tags, and links to the right places (if applicable).

My hope is that this site will act as a place where I can both see my growth, as well as showcase it to others.

This site is made in Hugo, a Go based static site generator. While I know the basics of Hugo, I decided against trying to reinvent the wheel and instead just used a premade theme. The theme used (much better than I could ever create) is Paper 6, and you can use the link in the footer for more information.
